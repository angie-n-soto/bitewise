# Scout Agent Skill

Use this skill when researching, searching, or discovering candidate pet food products, brands, and product lines online.

## Scouting Guidelines
- Gather target pet profile details (species, breed, age, allergies, budget, life stage).
- Filter search recommendations based on these constraints.
- Suggest candidates spanning dry kibble, wet food, and treats as requested.
- Detail basic product specs: brand name, product name, and main marketed benefits.
