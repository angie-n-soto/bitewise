# Vet Agent Skill

Use this skill when researching veterinary advice, medical guidelines, or academic literature.

## Scientific Grounding Guidelines
- Query and cite only trusted scholarly and veterinary domains (e.g., Google Scholar, AVMA, WSAVA, university veterinary extension articles).
- Ground recommendations in clinical research rather than commercial blogs or marketing brochures.
- Extract concrete parameters: recommended fat dry-matter ratios, specific therapeutic ingredients, or clinical study conclusions.
- Provide proper credits and citations for every medical claim made.
- Always note that recommendations are informational and do not replace consultation with the pet's own veterinarian, especially for pets with diagnosed medical conditions.
