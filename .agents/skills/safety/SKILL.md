# Safety Agent Skill

Use this skill when auditing the safety status of candidate pet food brands and products.

## Safety Recall Guidelines
- Query FDA and AVMA recall databases for any safety history related to the candidate brands.
- Scan recent news alerts for pet food contamination warnings (e.g., Salmonella, Listeria, Aflatoxin, metal fragments).
- If recalls are found, document the date, specific product/batch numbers, reason, and severity.
- Issue immediate safety warnings for active recalls and note any concerning patterns in a brand's safety history.
